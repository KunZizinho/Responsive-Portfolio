Little bit about me...

After I got my high school diploma, worked as a bartender in nightclubs of Croatia. 2012 I joined Carnival Cruise Lines where i stayed for five and a half years. Traveled all over the world as a bartender in a crafted cocktail bar.
After 11 years behind the bar, I decided to change my profession. In 2016, I passed 5 exams and received comTIA stackable certifications. the first 3 exams were not difficult since I had a background in computer hardware. I worked for 3 months as an intern, and after taking my last compTia security + exam I got a job at an MSP company. I spent just under a year working as a help desk technician for UCBOE.
Working on various projects as a help desk technician my attention was drawn to development. And since I'm passionate about creativity, I decided to enroll in bootcamp.

Personal Mission:

Front-End Web Developer leveraging a background in IT/Networking to build a more intuitive user experience on the web. Recently earned a certificate in Full Stack Development from Rutgers University, with newly developed skills in JavaScript, CSS, React.js, and responsive web design.

I applied aspects of UX and agile development in a recent project where I worked to develop a professional business website for a local Dental office.
Excited to leverage skills in the future as part of a fast-paced, quality-driven team in order to build better experiences on the web.
